About the Awards

The University Awards represent our highest tribute to excellence in education by our students and educating and nurturing talent, advancing knowledge and fostering innovation, and contribution to country and society by our Faculty.

Student Awards

Dean of Student Affairs Award

The Dean of Academic Affairs Awards is given to students

Dean of Academic Affairs Award

The Dean of Academic Affairs Awards is given to students who have demonstrated excellent academic performance in a year. These awards are given to those B.Tech students who have average SGPA of >9 in a year or whose average SGPA of two semesters is 2 more than the CGPA at the end of the previous academic year. For M.Tech students the average SGPA criteria for these awards is 9.5.

Honors Students

B.Tech students in IIITD can graduate with an Honors Degree. To be eligible for the same students have to do 12 extra credits, a B.Tech project and must have CGPA of 8 or more. Those eligible are awarded B.Tech with Honors.

Convocation Awards

Every year on Convocation, Institute awards Chancellor Medal and best BTP award to B.Tech students and best M.Tech Thesis awards to M.Tech students. The awards are given based on the defined criteria.

Faculty Awards

Teaching Excellence Awards

The Teaching Excellence Award is given to faculty who have demonstrated enthusiasm about their particular subject among the students by creating an exciting and inspiring learning environment for them. These awards are an outcome of semester wise nominations by the students.

Outstanding Educator Awards

The outstanding Educator Award acknowledges faculty who have excelled in engaging and inspiring students in their knowledge discovery. Each year the graduating batch nominates Faculty for this award.
